Gather

Find and gather the supplies you will need to wash your friend, be sure to find things that work with your dogs skin and do not cause any type of irritation. In this step you can also take the time to clean out the tub and get ready any hair dryers or towels you will need later.

Begin

Now to start cleaning your Shepherd. Begin by safely getting your dog into the bathing area. Once inside begin wetting the fur starting in the back at the legs and pants working towards dogs head. Begin soaping on the rear wet portion and scrubbing the grime off of your dog. After the rear is washed work towards the front and be carfeul of the face, eyes, and nose.
